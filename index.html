Cartridge Actuated Devices (CAD)

Cartridge Actuated Devices (CAD) is a leading manufacturer of precision-engineered safety and ejection systems for the aerospace and defense industries. With decades of experience, CAD delivers reliable, high-performance solutions trusted by military and commercial aviation customers worldwide.

Our innovative technologies include:

  • Ejection seat systems
  • Emergency escape systems
  • Rocket motor initiators
  • Specialty pyrotechnic devices

EMCORE Defense Optoelectronics

EMCORE Defense Optoelectronics is a premier provider of advanced optical and inertial technologies for defense and aerospace applications. Our cutting-edge solutions enable precision navigation, targeting, and sensing in the most demanding environments.

Key capabilities include:

  • Fiber optic gyroscopes (FOGs)
  • Inertial measurement units (IMUs)
  • Tactical grade navigation systems
  • High-reliability optical components
